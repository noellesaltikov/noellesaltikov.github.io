Fast Track to the Future: Charli’s Pop Rebellion

Charli XCX has never played by the rules — and that’s exactly why she’s rewriting them. As she charges into her next era with turbo-charged beats, chaotic glam, and unapologetic pop instincts, she’s turning the mainstream on its head. From underground icon to chart disruptor, Charli proves that the future of pop is weird, wild, and radically personal. Buckle up — she’s driving.

Pop Star, Internet Artifact, and Full-On Fantasy
